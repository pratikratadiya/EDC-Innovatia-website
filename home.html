INNOVATIA' 17
Innovatia is the upcoming fest being conducted by PICT-EDC to promote and boost innovative ideas amongst budding business minds. With a number of events in the offering and cash prizes worth more than 40k to be won, this is a great platform for students to present their startup ideas as well as test their business knowledge and skills. Also, in collaboration with IIT BOMBAY-EDC we are conducting a workshop on 'Entrepreneurship and business model competition'. If you are interested in business and entrepreneurship or have an idea which you think can shake the market, then we are providing you the ideal platform- here at INNOVATIA' 17!
PICT EDC
The Entrepreneurship Cell, PICT firmly believes that India needs the drive and innovation of young entrepreneurs. The Cell therefore aims at manifesting the latent Entrepreneurial spirit of the youth.
The Cell not only motivates the youth to start-up their own enterprise, but also makes efforts to provide them with the necessary resources to do so. Through our initiatives, we try to bring out your entrepreneurial skills and provide PICT students with the vital support system that a budding entrepreneur demands.
